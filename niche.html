NOV 14, 2022

"Music and rhythm find their way into the secret places of the soul"

~Plato


I was intrigued by the sounds created by my older sister, who was learning the violin when I was only five. My parents acquiesced to my request for lessons to make just as beautiful and melodious music. I think Johann Sebastian Bach is the best composer ever living and crafted two of my favourite musical pieces: Sinfonia in D major BWV 789 and Prelude in C major BWV 846. In 2020, I started piano, beginning with the piano pieces Sonata in C by Mozart and Moonlight Sonata by Johann Sebastian Bach.

2022

2022 was the worst year of my progress in music. With Teacher Teresa now married and with a baby, she had no choice but to stop our lessons. Without a violin teacher, I started my theory classes. I began studying piano and theory under Teacher Edwynya. However, I continue practising the violin now and then so that I do not end up forgetting how to play when deciding to join an examination again. This year, in August, I did my Grade 5 examination and got a distinction, keeping my reputation in the family for always getting a distinction for music exams.

2021

With the COVID-19 pandemic now worse than in 2020, all hopes of getting my violin examination for Grade 4 diminished. With the continued closure of borders, the Performance Exams continued, requiring four pieces instead of the usual three. However, this also called for no other components, like Aural. As Teacher Teresa recommended, I started preparations for my Grade 5 theory exam to continue with the Grade 5 practical.

2020

This year was supposed to be the year I got my Grade 3 Violin examination. However, with the shocking turn of events when COVID-19 came, I had no choice but to cancel my examinations. Because of the pandemic, I switched from physical classes to online ones, even violin ones. This was extremely difficult and hindered my progress. I decided to start preparations for the Grade 4 exams.
